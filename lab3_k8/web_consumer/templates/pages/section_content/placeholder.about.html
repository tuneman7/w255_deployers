Academic Underpinnings:

  • The academic underpinnings of this project are fundemental economic concepts as taught in most undergrad Business Administration or Econ programs.
  • We operationalize, visualize, simplify, and make accessible, the work of Nobel Prize Laureate and Yale Professor, Robert Schiller, the late Professor Karl E. Case of Wellesley University, and the late Professor John M Quigley of U.C. Berkeley.
  • This Data Science application is based on the aforementioned authors' three papers:
  • IS THERE A BUBBLE IN THE HOUSING MARKET?
  • COMPARING WEALTH EFFECTS: THE STOCK MAREKET VERSUS THE HOUSING MARKET
  • How Housing Booms Unwind: Income Effects, Wealth Effects, and Feedbacks through Financial Markets
