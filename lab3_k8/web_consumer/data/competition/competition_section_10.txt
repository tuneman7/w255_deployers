<h3>Composite Multi Signal Analysis Model powered Market Advice Module</h3>
Our composite model simplifies the market-level recommendation with a three indicator recommendation.<br/>
We then give users a easy to use visualization explaining in detail market indicators with bearing on the direction of travel for a 
housing market.  We give attetion to seasonal factors, explaing to users why, a market may move in a certain direction. Additionally, we provide
users access to the raw dataset and the predict dataset.
