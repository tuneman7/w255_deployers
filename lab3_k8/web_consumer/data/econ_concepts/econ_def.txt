Economic resources are scarce. Faced with this scarcity, we must choose how to allocate our resources. Economics is the study of how societies choose to do that. <br/><br/>Microeconomics focuses on how individuals, households, and firms make those decisions.<br/><br/>Macroeconomics focuses on large-scale or general economic factors, such as interest rates and national productivity.<br/><br/>We have limited time.<br/><br/>We have limited money.<br/><br/>Our needs however are unlimited.
